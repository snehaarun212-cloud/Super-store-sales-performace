 Super Store Sales Performance Dashboard (Power BI)
 
 Project Overview

The Super Store Sales Performance Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze business sales data and track key performance metrics. It provides insights into sales trends, customer behavior, product performance, and regional distribution to support data-driven decision making.


 Problem Statement

Businesses often find it difficult to monitor sales performance across multiple regions, categories, and customer segments. This project aims to solve this problem by creating a centralized dashboard that helps stakeholders quickly understand:

* Overall business performance
* Sales and profit trends over time
* Customer purchasing patterns
* High-performing regions and products

---

 Tools & Technologies Used

Power BI Desktop** – Dashboard development
Power Query** – Data cleaning & transformation
DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Calculated measures & KPIs

---

 Dataset Description

The dataset contains retail business sales information including:

* Order details (Sales, Profit, Quantity, Order Date)
* Customer segments
* Product categories and sub-categories
* Shipping modes
* Regional and state-wise sales data

---

Dashboard Features

 KPI Cards

* Total Sales
* Total Quantity Sold
* Total Profit
* Average Shipping Days

Sales Analysis

* Sales by Month (Year comparison)
* Profit trends over time
* Sales by Order Date

Customer Insights

* Sales by Segment
* Sales by Payment Mode

 Product Insights

* Sales by Category
* Sales by Sub-Category

 Regional Insights

* Sales by Region
* State-wise Sales Performance

 Interactive Features

* Region filter slicers
* Drill-down analysis
* Dynamic visual interactions

---

 Key Insights Generated

* Consumer segment contributes the highest sales
* Standard shipping mode is most frequently used
* Technology category generates maximum revenue
* Certain states contribute significantly to total sales
* Sales show steady growth over time

---

 Business Value

This dashboard helps organizations:

* Monitor performance in real time
* Identify profitable products and regions
* Improve sales strategies
* Support faster business decision making

---

 Conclusion

This project demonstrates how Power BI can transform raw retail sales data into meaningful business insights using interactive visualizations and analytical dashboards.
